Here's a summary of the relevant information regarding the "MagentaMobil Special M Young" mobile phone plan from Telekom Deutschland:

- **Availability**: Since September 6, 2019

- **Maximum Upload Speed**: 10 Mbit/s
- **Reduced Speeds after 5 GB Usage**:

  - Upload: 16 kbit/s

- **Cancellation Notice Period**: 1 month
- **Termination**: Can be canceled anytime after the minimum term with a notice period of one month.

  - With Smartphone: €24.95

### Additional Notes
- The pricing excludes any one-time hardware costs. 

This plan includes mobile access for telephony, SMS services, and internet access. For detailed terms and conditions, visit the provided Telekom website.
